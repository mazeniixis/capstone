More About Me

I'm legally Deaf from birth. My experience as a Deaf person in a Hearing World have taught me how to interact with others who do not communicate the same as I do.
I also recognized that communication barrier is a real thing, and I had to find workarounds. My preferred method is through written methods or sign language if the other person knows it.
With computers, the communication barrier is completely removed and I can freely correspond with others regardless of their language. Spoken words are not necessary as written words are best through computers.
Hence, my goal is to work with computers, because I love to solve problems, I find computer programming to be more to my likings, and I aim to be a professional developer/programmer.
Even though I'm Deaf, I still enjoy music, but I cannot hear spoken words.
